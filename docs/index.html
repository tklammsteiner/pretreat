Breeding and biotechnology for insect farming

email: thomas.klammsteiner(at)uibk.ac.at

This project is a collaborative effort of Thomas Klammsteiner, Carina D. Heussler, Heribert Insam, Birgit C. Schlick-Steiner, and Florian M. Steiner.


Project description

Testing various pretreatment methods for rearing substrates and their effect on the black soldier fly life cycle, larval gut microbiome, and substrate properties.
